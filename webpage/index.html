Tracking and Empowering Your Mental Wellness

Our Goal

Goal

Mental health is important - now, more than ever. Yet it remains one of the most neglected, overlooked aspects of our health. Enter MentalMatters: a daily resource that tracks your well being, happiness, and mental state in the form of a daily journal entry. MentalMatters will detect potential shortcomings in your mental health and assist accordingly. Powered by AI, daily reflections will evaluate your mood, health, and well being with you, keeping you on track to become the best version of yourself. MentalMatters also allows you to look back into the past, allowing you to clearly visualize trends in your happiness and state of mind, and ponder over past days. Get Started

Youth

Everyone are susceptible to mental health disorders - that's what makes it an global epidemic. But teens and young adults, especially students, are more vulnerable than ever.

Depression

Depression is a serious mental health disorder; It can cause extreme sadness and impacts daily life. Over 280 million people are diagnosed with depression worldwide.

Stress

Stress is your body's natural reaction to change. However, too much stress can be extremely overwhelming, and detrimental to your wellbeing. In the past year, 74% of people have felt so stressed they have been unable to cope.
